Melting Memories

I feel memories are out of my control. I cannot remember something I want to keep nor can I erase some moments I don’t want to remember at all. I often try to hold remnants of old memories, but it is impossible. I emotionally struggle with this experience. But, I realize that every moment of struggle is my life as well. Little by little, I learn how to harmonize with my past.
