Inner Child

The sad truth is that I lost not only my old memories, but also the way in which I used to perceive the world. In general, we have lost memories of the early stage in our lives, especially memories of being a baby. Images of babyhood that is commonly seen, such as a picture of colorful toys, fancy mobiles, and happy moments with pets could be far from actual babyhood memories, but illusions created by adults. Memories can be fully retrieved only if they are seen through the perception of one’s past self. In this work, I try to contemplate my memories through a genuine perspective of my past self. In this process, I can capture an appreciation that I had lost for a while seeing with fresh eyes.
